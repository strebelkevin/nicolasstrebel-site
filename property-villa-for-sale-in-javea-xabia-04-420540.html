About this property

Tucked away in a peacefull and established residential enclave of Jávea, this elegant villa captures the essence of Mediterranean living where space, privacy, and effortless comfort come together on a generous 1,500 m² plot.

From the moment you arrive, the property offers a sense of and exclusivity, surrounded by mature greenery and open skies, creating a private sanctuary just moments from the vibrant life of Jávea.

A Home Designed for Living and Sharing

The main residence unfolds over two levels, thoughtfully designed to balance openness with intimacy.

On the ground floor, multiple living areas invite both relaxation and connection whether it's quiet mornings with coffee, long lunches with family, or evenings that flow seamlessly from the kitchen to the lounge.

Upstairs, the villa reveals its private side:

four beautifully proportioned bedrooms and three bathrooms, including a spacious master suite, offer comfort and for both residents and guests. Each space is filled with natural light, creating a warm and welcoming atmosphere throughout.

Independent Guest House Freedom & Possibility

A standout feature of this property is the fully independent guest house.

This private retreat offers its own living space, bedroom, and bathroom perfect for hosting friends and family in complete comfort, or creating additional value through short or long-term rentals.

It's a rare combination: the ability to live privately, yet share generously.

Space to Create Your Dream Lifestyle

With its expansive plot and solid construction, this villa offers more than just a home it offers potential.

Whether you envision a contemporary luxury renovation, outdoor entertaining areas, a poolside oasis, or landscaped Mediterranean gardens, this property is a canvas ready to be transformed into something truly exceptional.

The Essence of Jávea Living

This is not just a property it's a lifestyle investment.

A place where:

mornings begin with sunlight and stillness

afternoons are spent outdoors, enjoying space and privacy

evenings are shared with friends, family, and unforgettable moments

A Rare Opportunity

Properties that combine space, independence, and versatility in Jávea are increasingly hard to find.

This villa offers exactly that a unique opportunity to create a refined Mediterranean home with guest accommodation, in one of the Costa Blanca's most sought-after locations.
