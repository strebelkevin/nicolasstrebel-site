About this property

Nestled in the sought-after Maryvilla neighbourhood of Calpe, this extraordinary Mediterranean retreat captivates from the moment you arrive. Breathtaking vistas stretch across the coastline, framing the unmistakable silhouette of the Peñón de Ifach as it emerges dramatically from the sea. With Port Blanco and the shoreline just minutes on foot, the best of coastal living is right at your doorstep.

The property unfolds over two floors, featuring a principal residence alongside a fully self-contained annexe together offering five bedrooms, two bathrooms, and two fully fitted kitchens. Whether you're hosting extended family, welcoming holiday guests, or exploring rental income, the layout adapts effortlessly to your needs. A garage, utility storage, and a sun-drenched solarium ideal for watching the sun melt into the Mediterranean complete the picture.

Outside, a generous terrace wraps around a private pool that seems to float above the sea, delivering sweeping panoramic views that never grow old. It's a space made for long lunches, evening gatherings, or simply doing nothing at all beneath the Spanish sun. The grounds afford genuine seclusion within a tranquil residential enclave, while a delightful local restaurant is conveniently close by.

This is a property of rare versatility a serene seaside escape, a shrewd investment opportunity, or the foundation for your very own Mediterranean lifestyle. Whichever path you choose, the possibilities here are as expansive as the view.

Your hillside haven above the Costa Blanca awaits. Get in touch today to request further details or to book an exclusive private visit.
