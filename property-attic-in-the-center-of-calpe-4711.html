About this property

Discover This Charming Penthouse in Calpe

Imagine waking up every morning in a corner of the world where the sun shines almost year-round. This wonderful penthouse in Calpe, in the province of Alicante, is an opportunity you won't want to miss. With its exterior design and well-maintained condition, this space offers comfort and style in every corner.

Spacious Living Areas

With three cozy bedrooms and two bathrooms, this penthouse is perfect for both families and those seeking a coastal retreat. The bedrooms are well laid out to ensure privacy and rest.

Private Terrace to Enjoy the Weather

One of the gems of this penthouse is its stunning 80 m² terrace, ideal for enjoying the pleasant Mediterranean climate. Whether for relaxing with a good book, hosting a barbecue with friends, or simply taking in the views, this outdoor space caters to all your needs.

Modern Amenities

The penthouse is equipped with heating and air conditioning to ensure your comfort in any season. Additionally, its design includes a storage room to keep everything organized and a fully equipped kitchen that makes cooking a pleasure rather than a chore.

Views and Location

Located in one of the most sought-after areas of Calpe, you will not only enjoy a cozy interior space, but also proximity to the beach, restaurants, and local shops. This penthouse offers access to a unique quality of life in a vibrant natural setting.

Facilities and Accessibility

The building features an elevator, and its design includes double-glazing windows to ensure a peaceful and comfortable environment. With internet connection, you'll always be connected to the outside world.

This penthouse is not just a place to live; it is an opportunity to experience the life you have always desired on the beautiful Costa Blanca. Don't miss this unique chance to invest in your future and enjoy every moment in this lovely corner of Calpe.
