About this property

Embrace the Villa Ibiza, a 160 m² villa set within an 800 m² plot, blending modern design with seaside sophisticationalbeit just inland. With 3 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, a dedicated laundry room, and a delightful connecting terrace, this villa wraps around a private swimming pool, creating an elegant indoor-outdoor flow.

Comfort is paramount with pre-installed A/C heat pumps, while the Terracotta-style façade nods to Mediterranean roots.

Built by Construcciones Z Sanchez, you benefit from comprehensive architectural design, permit handling, financing options, and rental or management servicesall backed by a solid 10-year warranty.

Monte Solana grants splendid panoramas of sea, mountains, and coastline, while being only minutes from Pedreguer's vibrant markets and cafés. Access to Denia, Javea, and major highways is smooth and swift
