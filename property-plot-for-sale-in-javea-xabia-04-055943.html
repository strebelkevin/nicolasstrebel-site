About this property

Looking for the perfect place to build your dream villa in Javea? This 1,060 m² buildable plot for sale in Rafalet, Javea offers an exceptional opportunity to create a modern home in one of the most desirable residential areas on the Costa Blanca North.

The urban plot is fully serviced with water, electricity, and internet connections available, making it ready for construction. It enjoys a peaceful setting with open views and excellent sun exposure, ideal for a private villa with a pool and Mediterranean garden.

Rafalet is a quiet and well-established urbanisation, conveniently located just a few minutes from Jávea Golf Club, the Arenal beach, and international schools. The area offers easy access to both Jávea town and Benitachell, providing comfort, privacy, and accessibility.

Whether you're an investor, developer, or future homeowner, this plot is a smart investment opportunity in a growing area of Javea offering space, views, and great potential for a bespoke Mediterranean lifestyle.
