About this property

We offer you EXCLUSIVELY a unique oasis just 2.5 km from the beach: double plot, sea views and access without stairs Tucked away in a quiet cul-de-sac in one of Jávea's most desirable residential areas, this exceptional villa offers privacy, panoramic views, and a rare level of comfort with step-free access throughout, all just 2.5 km from the vibrant Arenal beach. What really makes this property stand out: 1,674m² double plot, beautifully landscaped with lush Mediterranean gardens and low-consumption irrigation. The constructed area is approximately 300 m², offering generous interior spaces spread over three levels. Step-free access to all levels, although the plot has a gentle slope, a rare and valuable feature. Uninterrupted views of the protected green areas, the Bay of Jávea and the Mediterranean Sea. 4 spacious double bedrooms and 4 bathrooms, cleverly distributed over three levels for maximum comfort and privacy. Separate guest studio with en-suite bathroom, ideal for guests or use as a home office. Open-plan main living area connected to a cozy sitting area and a charming interior patio perfect for sheltered al fresco dining even on windy days. Large covered terrace with lounge area and round dining table for eight, surrounded by flowering bougainvillea and leading directly to the pool area. Swimming pool with Roman steps, fully equipped covered summer kitchen with shower, and large flat lawns with sun loungers and private corners. Large double garage, ample parking, and a second, step-free upper access to the property, offering uncommon flexibility and ease of movement. The house features classic Mediterranean architecture, is impeccably maintained, and has a solid structure. The garden has been carefully designed, combining natural beauty with low maintenance. Unless you're specifically looking for a modern cube villa, this home offers everything you need to enjoy the Mediterranean lifestyle in one of the most prime locations on the Costa Blanca.
