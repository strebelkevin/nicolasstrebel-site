Villa for sale in Jávea / Xàbia

3 bed 190 m²

About this property

We are delighted to present Villa Mila, a dream residence located in the exclusive Lomas del Rey community in Jávea, on the stunning Costa Blanca North. Construction began in May 2024, and this Mediterranean-style villa radiates timeless elegance, offering a serene escape in one of the region's most sought-after areas.

Boasting a pristine white facade with gracefully rounded corners, Villa Mila is accented by the natural beauty of hand-carved Montgó stones, tinged with warm red hues, adding an authentic Mediterranean touch. Inside, the villa features micro-cement finishes in the bathrooms and throughout the flooring, harmoniously paired with rust-colored, double-glazed aluminum windows for a perfect blend of luxury and comfort. The bedrooms are equipped with electric exterior shutters, while the bathrooms feature sleek black fixtures, lending a modern and sophisticated flair.

The heart of this home is a spacious, open-concept living and dining area, seamlessly connected to a fully equipped kitchen. Large glass doors open directly to the outdoor pool and sun-drenched terraces, creating an ideal space for indoor-outdoor living. Additionally, there is an adjacent laundry room and a guest toilet for convenience. The villa's sleeping quarters include three double bedrooms, each with its own en-suite bathroom, ensuring privacy and comfort. Underfloor heating and ducted air conditioning throughout the home provide year-round climate control. The property also offers two covered parking spaces and an independent 37 m² apartment on the lower floor, complete with a bedroom and bathroomperfect for guests or extended family.

Outdoors, the villa features a stunning 42 m² pool with a shallow 'beach' area, perfect for lounging and soaking up the sun. The pool is surrounded by 155 m² of terrace, part of which is shaded by an elegant pergolaideal for enjoying family dinners and Jávea's mild climate throughout the year. The villa's elevated position offers breathtaking views of the lush green mountains and the iconic Montgó, Jávea's most famous landmark.

Situated in an exceptional location, Villa Mila is just 5 minutes from a new international school set to open in December 2024, 6 minutes from the golden sands of Arenal Beach, and near a selection of fine restaurants. Lomas del Rey is a newly developed residential area equipped with modern infrastructure, including a sewer system, underground utilities, and contemporary homes. Additionally, the adjacent mountain forms part of the Granadella Natural Park, offering scenic trails with panoramic sea views.

To schedule a private viewing or receive further details about this exceptional project, please don't hesitate to contact us.
