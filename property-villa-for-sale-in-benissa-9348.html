3 bed 3 bath 181 m²

About this property

This magnificent villa, ready to move into, is located in one of the most sought-after areas of Benissa Costa (Montemar). It perfectly combines traditional Mediterranean charm with the comfort and technology of modern living. The property stands out for offering absolute privacy, a serene environment, and stunning sea views. A true gem in the current market, ideal as an exclusive primary residence or as a high-yield vacation investment.

Distribution and Spaces: The home consists of 181 m2 built, divided into two completely independent levels, set on a beautiful garden plot of 838 m2 with mature palm trees and low-maintenance native vegetation.

- Upper Floor (Main Residence): Houses the main living area with a cozy living room, bar area, a charming glazed naya (ideal as a dining room with views), two spacious bedrooms (one of them en-suite), and an additional bathroom.

- Lower Floor (Guest Apartment): A completely independent space that includes a bedroom, a comfortable living room, and a bathroom with a shower. It is the perfect option to host family, friends, or for rental purposes.

- Expansion Potential: The property offers the possibility of expansion by approximately 70 additional m2, ideal for building a private gym, a painting studio, or an extra bedroom.Exterior: The exterior is designed to enjoy the Mediterranean outdoor lifestyle. It features several terraces, shaded areas, and patios perfect for relaxing or dining outside. The main attraction is its 10x5 meter pool, which comes with a completely new filtration pump.

Sustainability and Cutting-Edge Technology: The villa has been updated with an ecological approach and maximum energy efficiency:

- Modern photovoltaic system of 4.4 kW with online battery storage of 5 kW, ensuring minimal electrical consumption.

- Gas central heating, additional infrared heating panels, and state-of-the-art air conditioning systems (cooling and heating) in living areas for optimal comfort year-round.Highlighted Features:

- Location: Benissa Costa (Montemar), maximum privacy and tranquility.

- Views: Spectacular unobstructed sea views.

- Area: 181 m2 living space (expandable by 70 additional m2) and a plot of 838 m2.

- Pool: Large private 10x5 meter pool with a new pumping system.

- Kitchen: Fully equipped modern design kitchen with high-end Siemens appliances (induction hob, grill, oven, dishwasher, and freezer).

- Comfort: Double-glazed windows with integrated mosquito nets and high-speed fiber optic internet connection.

- Parking: Covered carport for 2 vehicles and an independent storage shed.

- Furniture: Negotiable.An unique opportunity in a premium location. The property is immaculate and ready for immediate move-in.
