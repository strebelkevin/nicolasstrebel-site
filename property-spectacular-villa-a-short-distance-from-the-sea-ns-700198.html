About this property

Not all villas surprise you. This one does. Located on a spacious flat plot in La Fustera, Benissa, it combines Mediterranean character with modern design, offering panoramic views of the sea and mountains. Its location is as convenient as it is beautiful: just a few steps from La Fustera beach and the coastal path, and only minutes from the marinas, restaurants, and hidden corners of Moraira and Calpe. From the moment you arrive at its quiet cul-de-sac, the villa immerses you in a unique atmosphere. A majestic entrance, a wide driveway, and an independent double garage welcome you inside, where light floods a 100 m² open space with a designer kitchen featuring a central island and a magnificent glazed patio. Access to the main floor is smooth and step-free, making life here as simple as it is elegant. Stepping outside, you'll enter a private universe designed for unforgettable moments: a heated 14-meter swimming pool with a jacuzzi, stone terraces, relaxation lounges, and covered dining areas. Whether with friends, family, or simply with a glass of wine, this villa is made for maximum enjoyment. On the upper floor, the atmosphere is one of absolute relaxation. A VIP suite with stunning views of the Peñón de Ifach, two additional bedrooms with en-suite bathrooms, a Sky office, and a panoramic solarium complete the ensemble. With amenities like a 10 kW solar system, an intelligent security system, ducted air conditioning, and timeless exterior design, this villa offers not just style, but also peace of mind. This is not just any villa for sale on the Costa Blanca. It's a place where architecture, comfort, and the Mediterranean lifestyle perfectly merge: an elegant home in La Fustera that reflects you.
